About Me

My name is

Stephanie Archer

and I love dogs. I work full-time as a nurse; my passion is in dogs. I have owned and trained Boxer/Hound mix, Pit Bulls, Dobermans, German Sheppards and Labradors. Each was a rewarding experience in itself. I know each breed is different; I will work with yours to give them the individualized guidance they need. There is no such thing as a "bad dog"; dogs aim to please and want nothing more than to make you happy.
